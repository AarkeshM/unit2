Raghava Lawrence

29 October 1976 (age 47)

About:

Lawrence was born into a Tamil speaking family of Murugaiyan and Kanmani. Lawrence had a brain tumor when he was a child. He attributes the curing of his tumor to the deity, Raghavendra Swamy, and in an act of devotion, he took the name Raghava. He built the Raghavendra Swamy Brindavanam Temple at Thirumullaivayal on the Avadi-Ambattur route, which opened on 1 January 2010. Raghava also has a younger brother, Elviin Lawrence.
